Privacy Statement

This website only collects local data about email addresses voluntarily entered into our subscription service

This information is necessary in allowing users to subscribe to our club and get notifications in their inbox. This provides an accessible means via the user experience to register for news and learn more about other events, rather than making it indirectly accessible.

Data is stored locally, although it is set to expire by default every 6 months, unless a subscription is renewed. Email subscriptions are free.

Nonetheless, users have control over their data by being given the option to opt out of the subscription service/as well as being able to clear their data at any point using the "Clear Local Data" button. If left alone, these functions won't run and the website will continue to hold on to the user's personal email address.
